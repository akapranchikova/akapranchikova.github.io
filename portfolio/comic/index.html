I wanted to make a comic about Peter Parker
and his pursuit of Spider-Man. The main focus
of the comic is the hero's journalistic career
in the Daily Bugle magazine.
That's why logo includes elements related
to the video recording: red ellipse. All this creates
an atmosphere of surveillance and pursuit
of successful shots of Spider-Man.
To refresh Spider-Man logo from 2017, I have used
simple geometrical elements and simplified the form.
